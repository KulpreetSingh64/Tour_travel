    About Us

    Since 2005, Canada Travel has provided clients with personalized travel services to destinations across the world. Our story began over 10 years ago where Meena Thomas and Sheela Thomas found the need to help people plan their dream getaway. With combining their passion, they decided to partner together and run their own business creating Skylight Travel & Tours. Both Meena and Sheela bring over 20 years of experience in the industry and into the business. They then created a team where a select few of travel experts were recruited to join the team. The Skylight Travel family was finally complete and it was all history from there. Canada Travel is dedicated to the provision of objective, accurate, informative and reliable source in various sectors such as; international & domestic air travel, vacations, cruises and travel insurance.
